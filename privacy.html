Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025
Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights — in compliance with applicable privacy laws including the LGPD (Brazilian Federal Law No. 13.709/2018) and GDPR where applicable.
1

Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

  • Registration data: name and email address;
  • Usage data: pages visited, clicks, session duration;
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system;
  • TikTok account data: user ID, display name, profile picture, follower count, engagement statistics, and published video list — obtained via the TikTok API with your explicit authorization.
2

TikTok Data Usage

We access your TikTok data exclusively through TikTok's official API, after you grant permission via TikTok Login Kit. The data we access and how we use it:

  • user.info.basic — User ID and display name, used to identify your connected account within our platform;
  • user.info.profile — Profile picture and bio, displayed so you can confirm which account is linked;
  • user.info.stats — Follower and engagement metrics, used to power your analytics dashboard;
  • video.list — Your published videos, used to display content history and performance data;
  • video.publish — Used to publish scheduled videos to TikTok on your behalf;
  • video.upload — Used to upload video files as part of the automated publishing workflow.

We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose beyond the functionality described in this policy.

3

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our automation services;
  • Authenticate your account and connect to TikTok on your behalf;
  • Schedule and publish content as directed by you;
  • Display performance analytics and content history;
  • Send real-time notifications about publishing status via Webhooks;
  • Comply with legal obligations.
4

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us through our support channel.

Upon disconnecting your TikTok account from our platform, we will delete or anonymize all TikTok-specific data retrieved via the API within 30 days.

5

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements;
  • When required by law or legal process;
  • With your explicit consent.
6

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion of your data;
  • Withdraw consent at any time by disconnecting your TikTok account;
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.
8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through our platform's support channel.
